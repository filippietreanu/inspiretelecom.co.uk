Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my broadband transfer take?

After you have spoken with our amazing Broadband Team, they will process your order and send you a Broadband Welcome Pack, on this document it will state your Live Date for the broadband!

Will I experience any downtime during the transfer?

99% of the time, No. We bring your services over separate to ensure minimum downtime for you!

Will you cancel on my behalf?

If you're with Plusnet, EE, Vodafone, Sky or BT; we'll contact your provider and cancel on your behalf. If you're with Virgin Media, you will need to contact them.

I already have a router, why do I need a new one?

When you transfer to Inspire Telecom Broadband, we provide you a brand new pre-configured router. When this router arrives with you, it is already set up work with our network so all you have to do is plug it in and connect using the password on the back.

What if I already have a live phone line & broadband with another provider?

Call our UK-based support centre on 0330 122 9488. We will advise on the best possible steps to transfer over to us at no extra cost.

How will Covid-19 affect my broadband?

The continuation of COVID-19 in the UK has changed the way our company works for the safety of our customers and staff. During this time you may experience a difference in your broadband speeds, this is due to the increase in traffic through Open Reach. If you feel there may be an issue with your broadband, give us a call on 0330 122 9488

What network do you use?

We use the Openreach Network & Engineers. This means your service stays the same.

How does Inspire generates my bill?

Openreach record your usage and send it to Inspire on a monthly basis who generate you a bill & provide your improved customer service

Customer benefits

You receive a reduced monthly bill. Your Number, Line & Broadband remains the same. Plus... No In-contract Price Rises!
